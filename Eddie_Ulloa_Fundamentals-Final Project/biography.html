Claudio Domenicali

CEO of Ducati Motor Holding S.p.A.

Claudio Domenicali is an Italian engineer and business leader who has helped shape modern performance motorcycles. His work continues to push Ducati forward in innovation and motorsport.

Early Career

Joined Ducati in 1991 after studying Mechanical Engineering.

Racing Success

Led Ducati Corse — strengthening the brand’s motorsport legacy.

CEO in 2013

Guided Ducati through a new era of design and performance.

“Performance is not only about speed — it is about passion.”
— Claudio Domenicali
